Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password?

    • You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot password" on the login page.

  • Where can I find my billing information?

    • Billing information is available in the "Account Settings" under the "Billing" section.

  • How do I upload files for virus scanning?

    • You can upload files by navigating to the "File Upload" section of your account dashboard.

  • How do I contact support?

    • You can contact support through the "Contact Support" section or via live chat.
